About His & Hers Fashion Weekend

HHFW, as an annual fashion event, have evolved from Zinny Ladies Night Out in 2013, Zinny Night of Style in 2014 to our Style and Grooming Classes in 2016, then the HisAndHers event that took place at the Swiss Mabisel Hotel, Trans Amadi.

In 2018, it is now HisAndHers Fashion Weekend with a weekend show airing on the Silverbird Television Port Harcourt, every Saturday at 5pm called HisAndHers TV Series,with over 3000 members on our facebook group, you can get highlights of the show on Instagram, Facebook page/group: @hisandherstvseries
